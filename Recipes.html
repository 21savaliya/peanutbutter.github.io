Peanut Butter Smoothie Recipe

Peanut butter has a rich, creamy flavour and is high in protein, making it ideal for use in smoothies. You've certainly had a peanut butter smoothie at a restaurant or ice cream shop, but you might be surprised to learn how easy it is to prepare one at home. In no time, you'll be sipping a peanut butter smoothie made with just three ingredients and a blender. This simple three-ingredient recipe is ideal for unexpected visitors or a quick morning beverage for a healthy and full start to the day.

Ingredients of Peanut Butter Smoothie

  • 1 Banana
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 3 Tbsp Peanut Butter

    • How to Make Peanut Butter Smoothie

  • 1.Take a bowl and slice the banana in to it.
  • 2. Add peanut butter and milk to the banana.k
  • 3. Blend it thoroughly in a blender to make a shake.
  • 4. Pour it in a glass and serve it with ice.

    • Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe

    Peanut Butter Cookies Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/3 cup mashed banana
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 cup rolled oats (grind for 30 seconds in food processor)
  • 1 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

    • Peanut Butter Cookies: A Step-by-Step Guide

  • 1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees Fahrenheit and grease the baking sheets lightly.
  • 2. In a mixing dish, combine the honey and peanut butter.
  • 3. Whisk in the mashed banana and melted butter until thoroughly combined and then add the vanilla extract.
  • 4. Mix together the oats, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and chocolate chips in a separate basin. Combine the dry and wet ingredients in a mixing bowl.
  • 5. Using an ice cream scoop, evenly distribute the cookie batter onto the baking sheets.
  • 6. Bake the cookies for 15 minutes, or until the sides are just beginning to turn brown.
  • 7. Remove the cookies from the oven and place them on a wire rack to cool completely. Lightly season with flaky sea salt, if preferred.

    • Peanut Butter Yogurt Smoothie

    Peanut Butter Lassi is a delightful Indian beverage made with yoghurt, peanut butter, and fresh cream. Serve it as an after-school snack to your children.

    Peanut Butter Lassi is a refreshing protein-rich drink that is ideal for a warm afternoon or mid-morning snack. This lassi is a combination between a milkshake and a smoothie, and it's a nice and pleasant summer drink.

    Ingredients of Peanut Butter Smoothie

  • 1 cup curd (yoghurt)
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream
  • 4 teaspoons peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • ice cubes, if desired
  • salt to taste

    • How to Make Peanut Butter Lassi - Peanut Butter Yogurt Smoothie

  • 1. Gather all of the ingredients before beginning to make the Peanut Butter Lassi.
  • 2. Blend together all of the ingredients, including the curd, fresh cream, peanut butter, sugar, salt, and ice cubes, to form a smooth lassi.
  • 3. Adjust the consistency of the Peanut Butter Lassi with water if necessary.
  • 4. For breakfast, serve Peanut Butter Lassi with Palak Masala Bhurji and Bread Toast.
